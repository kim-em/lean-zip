## Key discovery

`split at h <;> try exact nomatch h` does NOT work in Lean 4 because
`nomatch` produces elaboration-level "Missing cases" errors that leak
through `try`. Use `split at h; next => exact nomatch h` instead for
batching error-case elimination.
