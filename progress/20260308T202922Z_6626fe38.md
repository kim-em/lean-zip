Added `decompressBlocksWF_two_compressed_literals_blocks` theorem to

compressed blocks (numSeq=0) produce `output ++ literals1 ++ literals2`.

## Decisions

- Used `exact` instead of `rw` for the second proof step because Lean's
  elaboration of `if let some ht := huffTree1 then some ht else prevHuff`
  produces `match huffTree1, hlit1 with ...` — a match on two discriminants
  that doesn't syntactically match the `if let` form. `exact` uses
  unification which handles this mismatch; `rw` requires syntactic matching
  and fails.
- Combined deliverables 1 and 2 from the issue: the docstring on the
  theorem serves as the content characterization note.
