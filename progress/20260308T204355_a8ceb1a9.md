  subsequent blocks, establishing the inductive step for multi-block frames
- **Two-block same-type** (#988): raw+raw and RLE+RLE produce concatenated output

- **Two-block compressed-literals** (#1000): compressed+compressed (literals-only)
- **Frame-level single-block** (#974): `decompressFrame_raw_content` and
