- **A.6 sentinel-gate residual-coverage note.** The duplicate guard
  is gated on the same sentinel predicate that gates
  `parseZip64Extra` itself. A crafted archive with two 0x0001 blocks
  but **no** standard-field sentinel bypasses both `parseZip64Extra`
  *and* the duplicate walker. This is documented intentional
  behaviour: the bullet at SECURITY_INVENTORY.md lines 274‚Äì323
  describes the three-guard layering; inline comments at
  [Zip/Archive.lean:535-539](/home/kim/lean-zip/Zip/Archive.lean:535)
  spell out that the duplicate guard runs "only when ZIP64 parsing
  is needed". APPNOTE's rationale is that a 0x0001 block without a
  sentinel is semantically a no-op, so duplicate no-op blocks are
  not a smuggling vector ‚Äî the resolved sizes/offset fall through
  to the standard 32-bit fields regardless. Outer
  `validateExtraFieldStructure` (#1788, unconditional) still
  enforces well-formed TLV layout on the no-sentinel path.
  Residual coverage: zero. Not a defect.

- **Sibling-closure note.** With #1785 (inner 0x0001-block `dataSize`
  exactness), #1788 (outer CD/LH sub-field overrun), and #1793
  (duplicate-0x0001 walker) all landed, the CD/LH extra-data
  layout-smuggling class is closed at the CD/LH boundary. The three
  guards are layered:
  1. `validateExtraFieldStructure` (#1788) ‚Äî unconditional, catches
     any sub-field whose declared `dataSize` overruns the blob;
     fires for *any* archive regardless of ZIP64 sentinel.
  2. `hasDuplicateZip64Extra` (#1793) ‚Äî gated on ZIP64 sentinel;
     catches two-or-more `0x0001` blocks before `parseZip64Extra`.
  3. `parseZip64Extra` inner `fpos == fieldEnd` exactness (#1785) ‚Äî
     catches trailing slack past `8 * N` bytes inside the single
     `0x0001` block that survives (1) and (2).
  Residual (intentional): duplicate `0x0001` blocks with no ZIP64
  sentinel (A.6) are semantically no-ops and not a smuggling vector.
  Writer-side `writeZip64ExtraCentral` / `writeZip64ExtraLocal`
  emit at most one `0x0001` block per entry, so none of the three
  guards fire on lean-zip-produced archives.
