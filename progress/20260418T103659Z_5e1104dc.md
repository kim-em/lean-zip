- **Pitfall #8 ‚Äî `unless` rebinds `mut` variables, dropping bounds.**
  Complements existing pitfall #7 (`if h : cond then throw` has no
  `¬¨cond` in the continuation). Even when `unless` does not assign
  to a `mut`, the elaborator threads the mut-state through it and
  emits a fresh `pos` in the continuation, so any captured bound
  (e.g. `hHdr : pos + 10 ‚â§ data.size`) no longer applies to the
  later accesses. Remedy: read every byte before any control-flow
  branch. Snippet derived from the `decompress` rewrite in
  `Zip/Native/Gzip.lean` (reading `id1/id2/cm/flg` up front before
  the `unless id1 == 0x1f && id2 == 0x8b` and `unless cm == 8`
  branches).
  Source: `progress/20260418T062850Z_5ac76a23.md`.

- **Pitfall #9 ‚Äî `List.pmap` (and other `@[expose]` combinators)
  break `rfl`-proved specs.** `@[expose]` makes the definition
  eagerly unfold on both sides of the equality; the kernel then
  recurses through the `pmap` skeleton simultaneously on each side
  and blows the recursion limit. Remedy: wrap the combinator in a
  named helper (the `freqsToPairs` trick already used in
  `Zip/Native/DeflateDynamic.lean`). Snippet mirrors the real
  helper.
  Source: `progress/2026-04-18T08-04-46Z_709ef861.md`.
