## Decisions and patterns

- **`simp only` vs `rw` for unblocking `split`**: the outer
  `match tokenFreqs tokens` bound via `let` blocked `split` from
  seeing the inner `if data.size == 0`. Switching
  `rw [lz77GreedyIter_eq_lz77Greedy]` → `simp only [lz77GreedyIter_eq_lz77Greedy]`
  triggered `reduceLet` and let `split` proceed. Worth adding to the
  `lean-simp-tactics` skill.
- **Bridge helper pattern**: when a hypothesis is stated in terms of
  `a[i]!` but the goal uses `a[i]'h`, introduce
  `rwa [getElem!_pos _ _ h] at hyp` to convert once, then reuse.
- **Private theorem vs inline proof**: because the EOB site appears
  twice (empty and non-empty branches), factoring to
  `deflateDynamic.lit256_lt` avoided duplication and kept the main
  function readable.
