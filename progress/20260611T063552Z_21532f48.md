## Landed (Zip/Spec/HuffmanEncode.lean, sorry-free)

- `kraftSum_perm` — `kraftSum` is permutation-invariant (via
  `List.Perm.foldl_eq'` with the +-right-commutativity discharged by `omega`).
  This is what lets the histogram-level Kraft equality transfer to the

- `blCountSum` (def) + `expandBl_length` — the number of lengths `expandBl`
  emits equals the histogram leaf count; `length_flatMap_replicate` is the

Builds on the prior session's `expandBl_kraft` (commit 9409615).

## Why decomposed (analysis for successors)

The full theorem needs three hard, coupled pieces:

1. **repairBl loop invariant** (→ #2543): each step lowers `blKraft` by
   exactly 1 *only if* `findBelow > 0` and `bl[maxBits] ≥ 1`. Neither
   follows from `excess > 0` — e.g. `bl[1]=3, maxBits=2` is over-subscribed
   with `bl[maxBits]=0`. Well-definedness needs a coupled invariant:
   conservation (`blKraft = 2^maxBits + excess_remaining`) + Kraft-prefix
   feasibility (`Σ_{l≤b} bl[l]·2^(b−l) ≤ 2^b`, `b < maxBits`) + leaf-count
   bound (`blCountSum ≤ 2^maxBits`).
2. **Invariant for cappedBlCount** (→ #2543): relate the capped histogram to
   the source tree's prefix Kraft sums. `cappedBlCount` is a `forIn` loop
   (opaque `loop✝`, per CLAUDE.md) — expect to refactor it to WF recursion
   first, then prove a binary-tree prefix-Kraft lemma off `BuildTree.kraft_eq`.
3. **Permutation argument** over `assignLengths (zip …)` (→ #2544): filtering
   the foldl-of-`set` result at distinct, in-range symbol positions = the
   multiset of assigned lengths. Combined with the landed lemmas this gives
   `kraftSum (filter nonzero output) = kraftSum (expandBl) = blKraft = 2^maxBits`.

Also: `repairBl` preserving `blCountSum` is *not* cleanly separable — its
Nat subtractions need `bl[maxBits] ≥ 1`, so it falls out of #2543's invariant
rather than standing alone.
