## Key decisions

- Added `br.bitOff < 8` precondition to `readBit_bitPos_eq` and
  `readBits_bitPos_eq`. Without this, the `bitOff + 1 ≥ 8` wrap case
  cannot prove `bitPos' = bitPos + 1` (omega produces a counterexample
  with `bitOff ≥ 8`). This is always satisfied in practice since
  `readBit` itself produces `bitOff < 8` (proved as `readBit_bitOff_lt`).
- The `readBit_bitOff_lt` helper enables the induction in `readBits_go`:
  each `readBit` call produces a reader satisfying the precondition for
  the next step.
