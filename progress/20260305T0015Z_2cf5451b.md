### Design decisions
- Omitted `resolveOffset_positive_repeat` (repeat offset codes 1–3): proving
  positivity for repeat codes requires bridging `Array.get!` and `Array.getElem`,
  plus the degenerate case (rawOffset=3, litLen=0, history[0]=1) genuinely returns 0.
  Left for future work.
- Omitted `executeSequences_output_length`: monadic `forIn` unfolding is hard
  without Mathlib. Marked as future proof work.
- Used `decide +revert` for concrete array proofs (Lean 4.29 feature).
