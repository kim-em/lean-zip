## Key Pattern Discovered

When `rw [decompressBlocksWF_compressed_sequences_step ...]` rewrites the goal, the step
theorem's proof (which uses `congr 1; cases huffTree <;> rfl`) introduces a dependent
`match huffTree1, hlit1 with ...` in the goal rather than the stated `if let some ht :=
huffTree1 then some ht else prevHuff`. These are propositionally but NOT definitionally
equal. The fix: `cases huffTree1` after the `rw` reduces both the dependent match in the
goal and the `if let` in `hlit2`'s type to the same concrete form (`some ht` or `prevHuff`).
