## Decisions / patterns

- The prefix `≤` bound needs the `d > b → cappedKraftSum = 0` helper
  (`cappedKraftSum_zero_of_above`): the naive `2^(b−(d+1))+2^(b−(d+1))` IH bound is
  too loose at the `d = b` node (saturating `Nat` sub gives `1+1` where the truth is
  `0`). The `≥` side has no such issue — looser lower bounds are fine.
- Hypothesis `∀ p ∈ t.depths 0, 1 ≤ p.2` is required so every increment lands at a
  level `≥ 1`; a depth-0 leaf would sit in bucket 0, outside `blKraft`. Real trees
  (`buildHuffmanTree`, ≥2 leaves) satisfy this via `buildHuffmanTree_depths_ge_one`.
- No-Mathlib gotcha: `by_contra` and `Nat.le_or_lt` are unavailable; used
  `Nat.lt_or_ge` + `Nat.min_eq_left/right` case-splits instead.
