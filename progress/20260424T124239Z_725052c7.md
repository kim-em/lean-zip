## Summary

Added the per-entry ZIP64 `versionNeededToExtract ≥ 45` lower-bound
guard in `parseCentralDir`, sibling of the existing per-entry upper
bound (PR #1807, `versionNeededToExtract ≤ 45`) and the archive-level
EOCD64 lower bound (PR #1764, in-flight). Together the three checks
pin the field to exactly the APPNOTE §4.4.3.2 permitted range and
eliminate a ZIP64-sentinel-mismatch smuggling vector: a crafted archive
with `versionNeeded = 20` and a ZIP64 sentinel in
`stdCompSize`/`stdUncompSize`/`stdOffset` is now rejected at CD parse
with `"ZIP64 entry with versionNeededToExtract"`, before
`parseZip64Extra` is called.
