## Key discovery

`Lean.Loop.forIn` (used by `while` loops) is `partial`, making the internal
`loop` function an axiom to the kernel. Range `forIn` (`for i in [:n]`) can
be converted to `List.forIn'.loop` via `Std.Legacy.Range.forIn_eq_forIn_range'`
and IS provable. But `while` loops have no such conversion.
