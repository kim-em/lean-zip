Closes #1823 — Track E: reject ZIP64 EOCD64 records whose
`versionMadeBy` spec-version byte exceeds `63`.

## Summary

Added the archive-level EOCD64 `versionMadeBy` lower-byte
upper-bound guard to `Zip.Archive.findEndOfCentralDir`. APPNOTE
§4.4.2.1 / §4.4.2.2 caps the lower byte at `63` (spec version 6.3);
values beyond that are either forward-looking extensions lean-zip
does not support or a crafted smuggle against strict readers.
Archive-level counterpart to the in-flight per-entry CD+4
`versionMadeBy` check (issue #1812 / PR #1820).
