### D.1 New dimension category — intra-CD invariants

This is a net-new category in the Track E coverage taxonomy: an
**intra-CD invariant** is a per-entry check that fires at
`parseCentralDir` time using only CD-resident values, independent
of any LH read. Inaugural row:

| Invariant                                       | Throws at                      | Gate                                                   | Direction       | Fixture                                 | Landed in |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| `method=0 ⇒ compSize == uncompSize` (stored)   | `Zip/Archive.lean:487`         | `parseCentralDir` loop (post-ZIP64-resolution)         | equality        | `cd-stored-size-mismatch.zip`           | **#1773** |

Architectural differences from the CD/LH dimension family:

1. **Gate layer.** Fires inside `parseCentralDir`'s per-entry loop,
   **not** inside `readEntryData`. Archive.list (which never reads
   LH payload) also catches the violation; the CD/LH family is blind
   to anomalies in `Archive.list`-only call paths.
2. **LH-free.** No LH read required — the check depends only on the
   CD-resolved `method` and the ZIP64-resolved `compSize`/`uncompSize`.
3. **Pre-I/O.** Fires before `readBoundedSpanFromHandle` (the
   compressed-payload read at `:820`), so a malformed fixture never
   charges against `maxEntrySize` even when the payload is multi-GB.
4. **Tautology vs. skew.** The check enforces a property intrinsic
   to method 0 itself (no compression ⇒ sizes equal), not a
   CD-vs-LH consistency claim.

| Fixture                                 | Angle                      | Trigger                                                       |
