## Technical notes

- `do` notation with `let mut` desugars into deeply nested `Bind.bind`/`Except.bind`
  chains. `simp only` can reduce these, but `split at h` picks innermost matches
  rather than outermost binds, making it impractical for manual decomposition.
- `grind` successfully handles the reduced bind chain for `accuracyLog_eq` but
  cannot reason through `forIn` loop iterations for `cells_size`.
- The `cells_size` proof needs: a general loop invariant theorem stating that if
  a property `P` holds on the initial accumulator and the loop body preserves `P`,
  then `P` holds on the result. This is a standard library gap.
