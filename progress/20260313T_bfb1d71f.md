identical 7-line proof body: unfold decompressFrame, thread parseFrameHeader, dictionary
check, unfold decompressBlocks, rewrite block result, checksum/size guards, exact.

Refactored 4 frame-level theorems in scope from 7-line tactic proofs to term-mode
one-liners. Net result: -18 lines (50 added, 68 removed).
