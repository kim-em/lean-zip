## Accomplished

Added two theorems to `Zip/Spec/ZstdFrame.lean` completing three-branch
coverage of `decompressZstdWF`:

1. **`decompressZstdWF_single_skippable_frame`**: When a skippable frame is
   at `pos` and the position after skipping is past the end of data, the
   output is returned unchanged (skippable frames contribute no content).

2. **`decompressZstdWF_skip_then_standard`**: When a skippable frame is
   followed by a standard Zstd frame, the result is `output ++ content`
   (only the standard frame contributes decompressed content). Composes
   the skippable frame spec with `decompressZstdWF_single_standard_frame`.
