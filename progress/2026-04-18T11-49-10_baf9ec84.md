## Key discovery: `split` tactic maxSteps cap

Deeply-nested `dite` (6+ levels, produced by the lazy-match structure)
makes the `split` tactic fail with "simp failed: maximum number of
steps exceeded". The step counter is internal to `split`'s simp call
(hardcoded maxSteps=100000) — it is NOT affected by
`set_option Meta.Tactic.simp.maxSteps` or `maxHeartbeats`.

**Fix**: add `set_option backward.split false in` before the theorem.
This switches `split` from the simp-theorem-based implementation to a
simproc-based one that avoids the step budget. Caveat: the simproc
variant may order cases differently, so anonymous rename_i hypothesis
names shift — add explicit `rename_i hX` at each level to stabilize.

Also: `set_option ... in` must come BEFORE `private`/any attributes
(Lean 4.29 rejects it between the docstring and `private`).
