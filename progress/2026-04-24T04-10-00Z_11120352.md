## D. Cleanup opportunities (proposed, gated)

**D.1 Fixture-generator deduplication — gate fails, not filed.**
`scripts/build-cd-lh-mismatch.py` is 128 LOC after #1728 (gate §D.1
opens: > 100 LOC). But the generator is already well-factored: four
helpers (`make_lh` 15 LOC, `make_cd` 21 LOC, `make_eocd` 9 LOC,
`write` 9 LOC) drive five one-call-per-fixture invocations of
`write(...)` with differing kwargs. The five `write(...)` blocks
average ~4 LOC each (a path plus 2–3 kwargs overriding defaults),
with a 2–5 line comment above each explaining the attack vector.
A table-driven rewrite would save ~10 LOC by folding the five
`write` blocks into a loop, but (a) the per-fixture comments carry
grep-able context about which guard each fixture targets, and (b)
the deduplication would be < 30 lines, which *is* inside the gate's
ceiling but is stylistic rather than structural. Net: the
structural dedup is already in place via the helper functions;
further consolidation is a mild readability trade and not worth
filing. No follow-up issue filed.
