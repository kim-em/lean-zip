### New lemmas added

1. **`allCodes_cw_length_pos`** (private): Codewords from `Huffman.Spec.allCodes`
   have positive length. Proof: `allCodes_mem_iff` → `codeFor_spec` → `natToBits_length`
   with the `codeFor` guard ensuring `lengths[sym] > 0`.

2. **`encodeSymbol_length_pos`** (private): If `encodeSymbol` succeeds on a
   flipped `allCodes` table, the result is nonempty. Uses `encodeSymbol_mem` to
   get table membership, then `allCodes_cw_length_pos`.

3. **`encodeLitLen_nonempty`**: Encoding one LZ77 symbol always produces ≥1 bit.
   Case split on symbol type; all cases reduce to `encodeSymbol_length_pos` on the
   lit/length Huffman table. No additional helper lemmas were needed beyond the two
   above.

### Proof repair

The `encodeSymbols_decodeSymbols` proof follows the same induction-on-syms structure
as the old fuel-based proof, with two key adaptations:
- **Unfolding**: `unfold decodeSymbols` instead of `simp only [decodeSymbols]`
  (WF functions diverge under simp)
- **WF guard**: `dif_pos hlen` where `hlen` uses `encodeLitLen_nonempty` + `omega`
  to show `(restBits ++ rest).length < (symBits ++ restBits ++ rest).length`
