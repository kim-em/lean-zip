## Key patterns discovered

1. **`simp only` at hypothesis doesn't auto-close on contradiction**: Unlike bare
   `simp`, `simp only [...] at h` will rewrite `h` to `none = some ...` but won't
   close the goal. Need explicit `exact nomatch h` after.

2. **Array lemma names for `simp only`**: `Array.set!` must be included in the
   lemma list to unfold before `Array.size_setIfInBounds` can fire. Similarly,
   `Array.size_extract` and `Nat.sub_zero` are needed for extract-based size goals.

3. **Guard chain reduction**: `guard` lemma is only needed for the first guard
   in a chain; subsequent guards after `beq_iff_eq` don't need it explicitly.
