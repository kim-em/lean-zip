## Decisions / notes

- Chose to count `range_checks` as every `:X-Y` anchor scanned,
  not just inverted ones — matches the verification text in the
  issue ("count of all `:X-Y` anchors scanned"). This differs from
  how `placeholder_checks` counts (which matches every
  occurrence including non-violations in practice because every
  occurrence IS a violation). Naming "range-anchor checks"
  (plural "checks", i.e. scans performed) is consistent with
  `zip_checked` and `quote_checks`.

  issue's scope-guard explicitly calls this out.

  environment; noted in the commit message. `bash -n` parses clean.
