## Accomplished

Closed the last remaining dimension of the CD/LH header-metadata
consistency family.  The DOS-encoded last-modified `UInt16` time/date
pair (LH +10/+12, CD +12/+14) is now threaded through
`Archive.Entry` as `lastModTime` / `lastModDate` fields (both
defaulting to `0`, preserving byte-identity for field-name-syntax
constructions), populated from the CD in `parseCentralDir`, and
cross-checked in `readEntryData` with
`"zip: lastModTime/Date mismatch between CD and local header for <label> (...)"`.

The check is a single combined `unless` on both fields and is
**ungated** — APPNOTE §4.4.6 restricts the bit-3 data-descriptor
allowance to crc/compSize/uncompSize only; the timestamp fields are
always carried in the LH.  Placement is immediately after the
`versionNeededToExtract` check and before the `usesDataDescriptor`
branch, matching the requested slot.

## Decisions

- **Combined vs split throw**: combined in one `unless` on both
  fields, as recommended by the issue — the two fields are physically
  adjacent and semantically a single DOS timestamp pair.
- **Check gate**: ungated (no bit-3 skip) since APPNOTE §4.4.6
  restricts bit 3 to crc/compSize/uncompSize only.
- **Fixture dimension**: single-dimension (time only, not time+date)
  to keep error attribution deterministic.
- **Rebase conflict resolution**: took PR #1761's simpler
  `set_option maxRecDepth 2048` as a top-level setting and dropped
  my own `set_option maxRecDepth 1024 in ...` on the function header.
