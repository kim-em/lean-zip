## Decisions

- Used `bv_decide` rather than manual Nat bitwise reasoning. The SAT
  solver handles 3 universally quantified 8-bit values efficiently (~1s).
- Kept `validBlockSize` predicate unchanged — it correctly describes
  the RFC 128KB limit, just not what `parseBlockHeader` enforces.
