## What landed

Added a CD-parse-time guard in `parseCentralDir` that rejects CD entries
whose `nameLen` (CD +28, UInt16) is `0`, with the error substring
`"CD entry has empty filename"`. Fires immediately after the `nameLen`
read and before the `entryEnd > cdEnd` overrun check and before the
sibling NUL-byte / path-safety filename guards — so failure attribution
pins to the structural anomaly rather than the path-safety predicate
(which would otherwise catch `""` via its empty-component rejection).
