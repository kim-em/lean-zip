## Key decisions

- **Proof strategy**: `decide_cbv`, `bv_decide`, and `grind` all failed on this goal due to
  mixed `Nat`/`UInt64` arithmetic (`.toNat` conversions between `UInt8` and `UInt64`).
  The winning approach was converting `UInt8` to `Fin 256` and using `decide` for exhaustive
  evaluation over all 256 inputs. Required `set_option maxRecDepth 1024`.

- **UInt8 → Fin 256 pattern**: `UInt8 = BitVec 8 = { toFin : Fin 256 }`. To enumerate all
  values: `have h : ∀ i : Fin 256, P ⟨⟨i⟩⟩ := by decide` then `exact h d.toBitVec.toFin`.
  Structure eta fires to unify `⟨⟨d.toBitVec.toFin⟩⟩` with `d`.
