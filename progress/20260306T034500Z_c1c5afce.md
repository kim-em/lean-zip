## Accomplished

Proved two mathematical property theorems in `Zip/Spec/ZstdHuffman.lean`,
reducing sorry count from 5 to 3:

1. **`isPow2_iff`**: The bit-trick `n > 0 && (n &&& (n-1)) == 0`
   correctly characterizes powers of 2 (`∃ k, 1 <<< k = n`).
   - Reverse direction: testBit extensionality — `2^k` has exactly
     bit k set, `2^k - 1` has bits below k set, so their AND is 0.
   - Forward direction: strong induction on n, case-splitting on parity:
     - Even n: helper lemma `land_pred_even` shows
       `n &&& (n-1) = 2 * (n/2 &&& (n/2-1))`, reducing to IH.
     - Odd n ≥ 3: helper lemma `land_pred_odd` shows
       `n &&& (n-1) = 2*(n/2) > 0`, contradiction.
     - Odd n = 1: direct (k = 0).

2. **`weightSum_pos_of_exists_nonzero`**: If any weight `weights[i] > 0`,
   then `weightSum weights > 0`.
   - Three helper lemmas: `weightStep_ge` (fold step ≥ acc),
     `weightFold_mono_init` (fold monotone in initial value),
     `weightFold_ge_init` (fold preserves lower bound).
   - Main proof: split list at index i, show processing the positive
     weight makes accumulator ≥ 1, remaining fold preserves this.
