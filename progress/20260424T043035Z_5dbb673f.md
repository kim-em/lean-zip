## A. PR summary

PR #1733 threads the EOCD-advertised entry count (`totalEntries`) out
of `findEndOfCentralDir` into `parseCentralDir` and, at the tail of the
CD walk, rejects any archive where `entries.size ≠ declaredEntries`.
This closes the CD/EOCD consistency category along its first dimension;
the trailing-signature loop in the pre-#1733 parser exited at the first
non-CD signature it saw, so an attacker could truncate the CD below
its advertised length and have the reader silently accept a shortened
entry list.

Delivered:
- `Zip/Archive.lean`: `findEndOfCentralDir`'s return tuple gains a
  `totalEntries : Nat` field; `parseCentralDir` gains a
  `declaredEntries : Nat` parameter; the new `unless entries.size ==
  declaredEntries` guard throws `"zip: EOCD totalEntries mismatch"`.
- `testdata/zip/malformed/eocd-numentries-mismatch.zip` — 122-byte
  fixture (EOCD declares `totalEntries = 2`, CD has exactly 1 entry).
- `ZipTest/ZipFixtures.lean` — one new `assertThrows` block plus the
  cleanup-list registration.
- `SECURITY_INVENTORY.md` — three updates (*Missing work* residual
  gap list, *Recent wins*, *Minimized Reproducer Corpus*).

Not delivered — correctly, not a scope miss:
- No `Local guard inventory for Handle.read and Stream.read` row for
  the new fixture. The `totalEntries` check fires *after*
  `readExact h cdSize "central directory"` returns, not before any
  `Handle.read`. It therefore does not gate a `readExact` call and
  belongs in the *Minimized Reproducer Corpus* only — see §C.3 below.

## D. Cleanup opportunities

**D.1 Fixture-generator consolidation — proposed as an optional
follow-up, not filed.** Post-#1742, the project has two
fixture-generation surfaces for CD/LH + CD/EOCD consistency fixtures:

- `scripts/build-cd-lh-mismatch.py` (128 LOC) — generates the 5 CD/LH
  fixtures plus `eocd-disknum-mismatch.zip` via four helpers
  (`make_lh`, `make_cd`, `make_eocd(..., disk_start=0)`, `write`).
- A 7-line inline-comment recipe in `ZipTest/ZipFixtures.lean:343–350`
  that regenerates `eocd-numentries-mismatch.zip`.

Folding the numentries recipe into `scripts/build-cd-lh-mismatch.py`
would (a) give the fixture the same deterministic single-source-of-truth
as the other seven and (b) give the script's `make_eocd` helper a
second overridable kwarg (`num_entries=1`) parallel to
`disk_start=0`. Net LOC delta: +~6 in the script, –7 in the
comment, so –1 LOC with stronger single-source guarantees. **Not
filed** — the inline-recipe style is acceptable for single-purpose
fixtures, and deprecating it would invite a broader refactor
(what about the `bad-crc.zip` / `bad-method.zip` / cd-past-eof
`481e562` fixtures, which have no inline recipe at all and no
regeneration script either?). A unified generator for the whole
`testdata/zip/malformed/` corpus would be a meaningful improvement,
but the scope is larger than a single follow-up issue warrants today;
noting it as a future meditation target instead. The current split is
correct but not ideal.

**D.2 Convention-consistency cleanup (pre-existing) — not filed.**
Line 507 of `SECURITY_INVENTORY.md` (the CD/LH-payload-read row) lists
regression fixtures inline in the `Local guard` column: six fixtures

Not filed.
