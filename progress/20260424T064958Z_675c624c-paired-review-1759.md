### A.1 Field read ‚Äî UInt16, no `.toNat`

[`Zip/Archive.lean:444`](/home/kim/lean-zip/Zip/Archive.lean:444)
reads

    let diskNumberStart := Binary.readUInt16LE data (pos + 34)

immediately after the three existing 2-byte reads at
`pos + 28 / 30 / 32` (`nameLen`, `extraLen`, `commentLen` at
[`:433`](/home/kim/lean-zip/Zip/Archive.lean:433)‚Äì[`:435`](/home/kim/lean-zip/Zip/Archive.lean:435)),
where only `diskNumberStart` is **not** then `.toNat`-converted.
The check at [`:445`](/home/kim/lean-zip/Zip/Archive.lean:445) is

`Nat` cast. Optimal ‚Äî no widening, no allocations on the happy path,
and the error message at [`:447`](/home/kim/lean-zip/Zip/Archive.lean:447)
interpolates `{diskNumberStart}` directly (the `UInt16` `toString`
suffices for attribution). ‚úì

### A.2 Check placement ‚Äî before span check

The new block sits between the `commentLen` read at
[`:435`](/home/kim/lean-zip/Zip/Archive.lean:435) and the
