## Accomplished

Added two content characterization theorems for `decompressBlocksWF` in `Zip/Spec/Zstd.lean`:

1. **`decompressBlocksWF_single_raw`**: When the block loop encounters a single raw last block, the result is `output ++ block` at the raw block's end position.

Both proofs use `simp only` with direct hypothesis rewriting to unfold the WF function and resolve all monadic binds in a single step, rather than the `split at h` / `exact nomatch h` pattern used by existing structural proofs.
