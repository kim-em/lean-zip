## Decisions

- Used `this_disk=99` (matching the issue body's recommended choice).
  ZIP64 EOCD64 hardcodes `thisDisk=0` in `make_eocd64`, and a stock
  single-disk archive is genuinely on disk 0; `99` is a non-sentinel
  UInt16 that numerically disagrees with the ZIP64 override, avoiding
  the "numeric match" relaxation lean-zip applies for Go's
  `archive/zip` interop (see `testdata/zip/interop/go-zip64.zip`).
