  functional `Bool` validator over a CD/LH extra-data blob that walks
  APPNOTE §4.5 sub-field headers (`[headerId : UInt16][dataSize : UInt16]

  `"malformed extra field for <name>"`; LH site emits
  `"malformed local extra field for <label>"` — distinct from the
  existing `"malformed ZIP64 extra field"` wording, which now fires only
