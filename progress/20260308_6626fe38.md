## Key Pattern Discovered

For BitReader `readBit` proofs after `obtain ⟨_, rfl⟩`, the struct literal
substitution means `dsimp only` can reduce all field projections (`.data`,
`.pos`, `.bitOff`), then `omega` closes the resulting arithmetic goals —
including disjunctions like `0 = 0 ∨ pos + 1 < data.size`. This replaces
verbose `simp_all only` calls with 10+ lemmas.

For conjunction goals including `data = data`, use
`exact ⟨rfl, by omega, by omega⟩` after `dsimp only` since `omega` can't
prove ByteArray equality.
